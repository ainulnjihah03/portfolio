A selection of my mobile application projects, showcasing my skills in creating intuitive and high-performance cross-platform apps using modern frameworks and robust backend systems.

De Barber Queue System

A mobile application designed to revolutionize the customer experience in barbershops. This app allows customers to book appointments, join a virtual queue, and check estimated wait times from anywhere. It eliminates the need for physical waiting, manages staff schedules efficiently, and sends automated notifications. The system enhances operational efficiency for barbers while providing a seamless and modern convenience for clients.

Hardware Deployment Coordination System (Mobile)

A mobile application designed for field technicians and vendors to manage hardware deployment and installation tasks. The app provides real-time job notifications from administrators, allowing vendors to accept or decline assignments directly from their mobile devices. It includes features for updating job status with photo proof, logging work hours, and communicating with the back-office team. This tool streamlines the deployment workflow, improves accountability, and ensures projects are completed on time and to specification.
